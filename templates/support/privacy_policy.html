Privacy Policy

We value your trust and are committed to protecting your personal information. Please read this policy carefully to understand how we handle your data.

This Privacy Policy describes Our policies and procedures on the collection, use and disclosure of Your information when You use the Service and tells You about Your privacy rights and how the law protects You.

1. Information We Collect

We collect several different types of information for various purposes to provide and improve our Service to you:

  • Personal Data: While using our Service, we may ask you to provide us with certain personally identifiable information that can be used to contact or identify you ("Personal Data"). This may include Email address, First name and last name, Cookies and Usage Data.
  • Resume Data: Information you input into the resume builder, including work history, education, skills, and personal summaries.
  • Usage Data: We may also collect information how the Service is accessed and used ("Usage Data").

2. How We Use Your Data

ATS Resume Builder Pro uses the collected data for various purposes:

  • To provide and maintain the Service
  • To notify you about changes to our Service
  • To allow you to participate in interactive features of our Service when you choose to do so
  • To provide customer care and support
  • To provide analysis or valuable information so that we can improve the Service
  • To monitor the usage of the Service
  • To detect, prevent and address technical issues

3. Data Security

The security of your data is important to us, but remember that no method of transmission over the Internet, or method of electronic storage is 100% secure. While we strive to use commercially acceptable means to protect your Personal Data, we cannot guarantee its absolute security.

We use AES-256 encryption for all sensitive data at rest and TLS 1.3 for data in transit.

4. AI Processing

Our Service uses Artificial Intelligence (Google Gemini API) to provide resume suggestions and optimizations. When you use AI features:

  • The text you provide (e.g., job description, resume content) is sent to the AI provider for processing.
  • We do not use your personal data to train public AI models.
  • Data processed by AI is ephemeral and is not stored by the third-party provider for their own use.

5. Third-Party Services

We may employ third party companies and individuals to facilitate our Service ("Service Providers"), to provide the Service on our behalf, to perform Service-related services or to assist us in analyzing how our Service is used.

6. Your Data Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access, update or to delete the information we have on you.
  • Request a copy of your personal data in a structured, machine-readable format.
  • Opt-out of marketing communications.

7. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us:

  • By email: support@atsresumepro.com
  • By visiting this page on our website: Contact Us
