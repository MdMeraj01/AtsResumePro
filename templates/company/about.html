Our Journey: From Frustration to Innovation

It all started in 2020 when our founders, experienced tech professionals, faced repeated rejections from automated hiring systems despite having strong qualifications. We realized the problem wasn't their skills, but how resumes were being parsed by Applicant Tracking Systems (ATS).

After months of research and development, we launched ATS Resume Builder Pro with one mission: to level the playing field for all job seekers. Today, we've helped over 50,000 professionals land their dream jobs with resumes that get noticed.
